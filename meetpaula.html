Meet Paula

Hello, I'm Paula

I offer an online counselling service combining humanistic counselling (person centred) with CBT (cognitive behavioural therapy) strategies to help clients change their lives for the better.

Before I became a counsellor I found myself struggling with the changes and challenges that came into my life, and life was no longer familiar, I didn’t know where to turn, I struggled with life and my issues for a couple of years. Eventually I found the courage to talk to a counsellor, obviously confidentiality was very important to me.

Facts about me

I am an accredited member of the BACP (British association for counselling and psychotherapy).
I am a registered counsellor with this governing body and a full member. I have specific online counselling training and have more than 13 years of UK qualification experience. I have worked for several crisis helplines, I have also been a volunteer counsellor with the NSPCC.
My goal is to help as many people as possible to reach their ‘full potential’ and help them to find inner peace and happiness.

Benefits of working with an online counsellor

  • Specific online counselling to suit you.
  • Ease of access.
  • Confidential.
  • Easy secure payment method, guaranteed by PayPal.
  • Read back the session content at your own convenience.

My Professional Qualifications

  • I qualified and was licensed to work as a counsellor in Nottingham, England, UK in 2003
  • Advanced Diploma - Working with Trauma-PTSD
  • Advanced Diploma – Person Centred Counselling: The Humanistic Theory
  • Advanced Diploma – The Developing Counselling Practitioner
  • Diploma - CBT - Cognitive Behavioural Therapy

My Promise

Whatever your problem is, whether its one big problem or a whole host of smaller, niggling problems, I will help you. Having someone to really listen to you helps a lot.

Using my 13 years experience of actually working as a counsellor, using tried and tested counselling skills to help you find your way again, I will help you to take control of your life, starting today.
